About Us

Real People, Real Solutions, Real Results

Our Story

ICU Media Design started in July 2024 out of a simple frustration: small Kiwi businesses kept getting told they needed "a digital presence" — then handed a five-figure quote and a contract full of jargon.

We thought there was a better way for the businesses we'd actually want to work with — clearer language, fairer pricing, and the work of someone who's actually invested in the outcome. Not someone juggling forty accounts at once.

Our Values

Clarity

Plain English, every step If we can't explain what we're doing in a sentence, we shouldn't be charging for it. No jargon, no smoke, no surprises in the invoice.

Partnership

Your wins are our wins We don't hand off a website and disappear. We stay involved while you grow into it — the long game, not just the launch.

Craftsmanship

The same care, every project Whether you're a sole trader or an established team, the same dedication goes into every line of code and pixel of design.
