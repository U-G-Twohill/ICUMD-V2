Our Story

ICU Media Design was founded in July 2024 with a singular mission: to empower small businesses across New Zealand with exceptional, user-friendly websites, services and solutions. As a locally-based company, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities faced by small Kiwi businesses whether local or international, and we are dedicated to helping them succeed in the digital landscape.

Our vision is to be the go-to web development and enhancement agency for small businesses in New Zealand, known for our innovative solutions, reliable service, and customer-centric approach. We believe in integrity, inovation, and excellence, and these values are at the core of everything we do.
