Common Questions About Web Design

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on complexity. A simple one-page or starter site takes 1–3 weeks, a business or service site 3–5 weeks, and online stores or custom projects 4–8+ weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline after our initial discovery call.

How much does a website cost?

Starter sites begin around $1,500 NZD. Business and service websites typically range from $2,500–$6,000, while e-commerce sites start from $4,000+. Custom projects are quoted individually. All prices exclude GST.

Will I be able to update my site myself?

Yes. We build on platforms like WordPress, Webflow, or Shopify and provide full training so you can make updates yourself. You'll also get documentation and video guides.

What's included in a maintenance plan?

Our maintenance plans cover security updates, backups, performance monitoring, and support — starting at $200/month for Basic, $400/month for Medium, and $750/month for Professional. Check out the plans above for full details.
