🟦 General Questions

Q1: What does ICU Media Design do?

We’re a full-service web and brand design studio based in Aotearoa New Zealand. We offer:

  • Custom Website Design & Development
  • E-commerce Solutions (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.)
  • Mobile-Friendly & Responsive Design
  • Branding, Logo & Visual Identity
  • SEO Setup & Optimization
  • Ongoing Website Support & Maintenance
  • Hosting and Domain Services
  • Digital Marketing Guidance

We work with startups, sole traders, community groups, NGOs, and small-to-medium-sized businesses here in New Zealand and overseas.

Q2: I’m a small NZ business—do you cater to my needs?

Absolutely. We love working with local Kiwi businesses and understand the unique challenges of starting and scaling online here. Whether you’re running a café in Christchurch, a tradie service in Wellington, or an online store from Nelson, we’ll tailor the design and features to your goals and budget.

Q3: Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We’ve proudly worked with clients across Australia, the UK, the US, and beyond. We schedule virtual meetings around your time zone and ensure payment and project timelines work for all parties.

Q4: How do I get started?

Just reach out via our contact form, email hello@icumediadesign.com, or call us directly. We’ll book a free discovery call to understand your goals and recommend a clear path forward.

🟩 Website Design & Development

Q5: How long does it take to build a website?

Our average turnaround times are:

  • One-page or starter sites: 1–3 weeks
  • Brochure/business sites: 3–5 weeks
  • Online stores & custom builds: 4–8+ weeks

Project speed depends on complexity, content readiness, and how quickly we receive client feedback.

Q6: Do I need to provide content and photos?

Yes, ideally. We encourage you to supply your own copy, photos, and brand materials. If you need help, we offer:

  • Copywriting
  • Photography sourcing or local photographer recommendations (NZ only)
  • Brand development services

Q7: Will I be able to update my site myself?

Yes. We build our websites on easy-to-use platforms like WordPress, Webflow, and Shopify. You’ll receive training or how-to videos so you feel confident updating text, images, and products.

Q8: Will my site work on phones and tablets?

Definitely. All sites are mobile-optimized and tested across devices. This is a top priority for us—especially since over half of NZ’s internet traffic comes from mobile users.

🟨 Pricing, Payment & GST

Q9: How much does a website cost?

Here’s a general guide:

  • Starter sites: from NZD $1,500
  • Business/Service websites: $2,500–$6,000
  • E-commerce websites: from $4,500
  • Custom builds: Quoted per project

All prices include GST for NZ clients. We tailor quotes to your exact needs with no hidden costs.

Q10: What are your payment terms?

For most projects:

  • 50% deposit to start
  • 25% after design approval
  • 25% just before going live

Payment can be made via NZ bank transfer, credit/debit card, or PayPal (for international clients). We also offer staged billing or retainers for long-term work.

Q11: Do you charge GST?

Yes, 15% GST is included for New Zealand-based clients. International clients are not charged GST.

Q12: Do you offer refunds?

Due to the custom nature of our work, we don’t offer refunds once the project has started. However, we’re committed to client satisfaction and will work with you to resolve any concerns.

🟧 Hosting, Support & Security

Q13: Do you provide website hosting?

Yes. We offer reliable, New Zealand-based hosting options or can set you up on platforms like SiteGround, Cloudways, or with your preferred provider. All our hosting includes SSL certificates and daily backups.

Q14: What happens if something breaks?

If you’re on a Support or Maintenance Plan, we’ll handle it ASAP. Plans include:

  • Regular backups
  • Plugin/theme updates
  • Uptime monitoring
  • Security patches
  • Quick fixes

Clients without a plan can contact us for one-off support.

Q15: Do you offer emergency support?

Yes. If your site is down or compromised, email urgent@icumediadesign.com with “URGENT” in the subject. We aim to respond within 2 business hours (NZT).

🟥 E-Commerce Websites

Q16: What platforms do you build online stores on?

We primarily work with:

  • Shopify (great for product-based businesses)
  • WooCommerce (for WordPress users)
  • Squarespace Commerce
  • Webflow E-commerce

We help you choose based on what’s best for your business and future plans.

Q17: Can you help with payment setup?

Yes. We support integrations for:

  • Stripe, PayPal, POLi, Laybuy, Afterpay, Windcave, and more
  • Local and international shipping plugins
  • NZ tax/GST rules setup

Q18: Will I get training on how to manage my store?

Absolutely. We provide tutorials, how-to videos, and optional 1-on-1 Zoom training so you feel confident running your online shop.

🟪 Branding & Graphic Design

Q19: What’s included in your branding packages?

Branding packages can include:

  • Custom logo design
  • Brand colour palette and typography
  • Social media assets
  • Business card design
  • Brand guideline PDF
  • Optional signage/flyer design (great for local NZ marketing)

Q20: Can I just get a logo?

Of course. We offer logo-only packages starting at $500 NZD, including multiple concepts, revisions, and all file formats for print and web.

🟫 Revisions, Communication & Process

Q21: How many revisions do I get?

Most packages include:

  • 2–3 rounds of design revisions

Extra rounds can be added at an hourly rate or built into your quote.

Q22: How do we communicate during the project?

We work how you work. Most communication is via email and Zoom, but we also offer Slack access, Notion client portals, and Trello boards for bigger projects.

For local NZ clients, we can arrange phone calls or in-person meetings depending on location.

Q23: Do you outsource work?

No. Our team is 100% in-house or trusted long-term collaborators. All work is reviewed by ICU Media Design for quality and consistency.

🟨 Ongoing Support & Aftercare

Q24: What if I need help after launch?

We offer:

  • Ongoing Support Plans (monthly)
  • On-demand edits and updates
  • Performance monitoring
  • SEO checkups

We’ll never leave you hanging once your site goes live.

Q25: Can you update my existing website?

Yes, we offer:

  • Website audits
  • Redesigns
  • Content updates
  • Speed/security fixes

Even if we didn’t build it originally—we’ll clean it up or rebuild as needed.
