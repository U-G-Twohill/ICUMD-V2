Getting Started

What does ICU Media Design do?

We’re a full-service web and digital solutions studio based in Aotearoa New Zealand. We offer custom website design, e-commerce, branding, SEO, business integrations, workflow automations, ongoing support, and hosting. We work with startups, sole traders, community groups, and SMBs in NZ and overseas.

I’m a small NZ business — do you cater to my needs?

Absolutely. We love working with local Kiwi businesses and understand the unique challenges of starting and scaling online here. We’ll tailor the design and features to your goals and budget.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! We’ve worked with clients across Australia, the UK, the US, and beyond. We schedule virtual meetings around your time zone and ensure payment and project timelines work for all parties.

How do I get started?

Just reach out via our contact form, email hello@icumediadesign.com, or call us directly. We’ll book a free discovery call to understand your goals.

Can you update my existing website?

Yes — website audits, redesigns, content updates, and speed/security fixes. Even if we didn’t build it originally, we’ll clean it up or rebuild as needed.

Web Design & Development

How long does it take to build a website?

One-page or starter sites: 1–3 weeks. Business sites: 3–5 weeks. Online stores & custom builds: 4–8+ weeks. Speed depends on complexity, content readiness, and feedback turnaround.

Do I need to provide content and photos?

Ideally, yes. We encourage you to supply your own copy, photos, and brand materials. If you need help, we offer copywriting, photography sourcing, and brand development services.

Will I be able to update my site myself?

Yes. We build on platforms like WordPress, Webflow, and Shopify. You’ll receive training or how-to videos so you feel confident updating text, images, and products.

Will my site work on phones and tablets?

Definitely. All sites are mobile-optimised and tested across devices — especially since over half of NZ’s internet traffic comes from mobile users.

Solutions & Automations

What kinds of business tasks can you automate?

Lead follow-ups, booking confirmations, email campaigns, content scheduling, invoice reminders, data syncing between tools, and much more. If you’re doing it by hand and it feels repetitive, there’s probably a smarter way.

What tools and platforms do you integrate with?

Google Workspace, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Calendly, Stripe, Xero, Slack, Notion, Zapier, Make, and many more. If it has an API, we can probably connect it.

Do I need a new website to use your solutions service?

No. Our solutions work with any existing website. We can also pair them with a new build if you’re starting from scratch.

How much do your solutions cost?

Simple integrations from $300 setup. Workflow automations from $500. Monthly management also available. We’ll scope everything in a free discovery call — no surprises.

Will I understand how to use the tools you set up?

Yes. Full training and documentation included. We build for handover, not dependency.

Pricing & Payments

How much does a website cost?

Starter sites from NZD $1,500. Business/service websites $2,500–$6,000. E-commerce from $4,500. Custom builds quoted per project. All prices include GST for NZ clients.

What are your payment terms?

50% deposit to start, 25% after design approval, 25% before going live. Payment via NZ bank transfer, credit/debit card, or PayPal (international). We also offer staged billing or retainers.

Do you charge GST?

Yes, 15% GST is included for New Zealand-based clients. International clients are not charged GST.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the custom nature of our work, we don’t offer refunds once the project has started. However, we’re committed to client satisfaction and will work with you to resolve any concerns.

Hosting, Support & Maintenance

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes. We offer reliable, New Zealand-based hosting options or can set you up on platforms like SiteGround, Cloudways, or your preferred provider. All hosting includes SSL certificates and daily backups.

What support do you offer after launch?

Ongoing maintenance plans, regular backups, security patches, plugin updates, uptime monitoring, performance and SEO checkups, and on-demand edits. Clients without a plan can contact us for one-off support.

Do you offer emergency support?

Yes. If your site is down or compromised, email urgent@icumediadesign.com with “URGENT” in the subject. We aim to respond within 2 business hours (NZT).

Working With Us

What platforms do you build online stores on?

Shopify, WooCommerce, Squarespace Commerce, and Webflow E-commerce. We help you choose based on what’s best for your business.

Can you help with payment setup?

Yes — Stripe, PayPal, POLi, Laybuy, Afterpay, Windcave, and more. Plus local/international shipping plugins and NZ GST rules.

Will I get training on how to manage my store?

Absolutely. We provide tutorials, how-to videos, and optional 1-on-1 Zoom training so you feel confident running your online shop.

What’s included in your branding packages?

Custom logo design, brand colour palette and typography, social media assets, business card design, brand guideline PDF, and optional signage/flyer design.

Can I just get a logo?

Of course. Logo-only packages start at $500 NZD, including multiple concepts, revisions, and all file formats for print and web.

How many revisions do I get?

Most packages include 2–3 rounds of design revisions. Extra rounds can be added at an hourly rate or built into your quote.

How do we communicate during the project?

Most communication is via email and Zoom. We also offer Slack access, Notion client portals, and Trello boards. Local NZ clients can arrange phone calls or in-person meetings.

Do you outsource work?

No. Our team is 100% in-house or trusted long-term collaborators. All work is reviewed by ICU Media Design for quality and consistency.
