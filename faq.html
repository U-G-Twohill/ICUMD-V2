Pricing & Payments

Hosting, Support & Maintenance

What happens after your site goes live.

We help you choose and set up hosting on the platform best suited to your needs — typically Hostinger for NZ small businesses. SSL and regular backups come with most modern hosting plans, and we'll walk you through what's included before you sign up.

Ongoing support scoped to what the site actually needs — backups, security patches, plugin updates, general upkeep and on-demand edits. If you'd rather not have ongoing support, one-off help is fine too.

If your site is down or compromised, email uriah@icumediadesign.co.nz with "URGENT" in the subject. We'll respond as quickly as we can — turnaround depends on the day, but you'll always hear back.
