## 3. What's possible — APPROVED

**Heading:** What's possible
**Sub:** A glimpse of what's within reach now — not a menu to pick from. The right mix is yours to find.

- **Get found.** Show up when your customers are searching — backed by real SEO and an honest look at what's already working.
- **Get customers.** Turn interest into booked-in, paid-up customers — enquiries and bookings captured and followed up, confirmed automatically or only once you approve.
- **Get time back.** The repetitive admin that eats your evenings — handled, so you can focus on the work you actually enjoy.

*(Bucket names chosen for universality across trade/retail/hospitality/creative — "win the work" was too trades-specific. Same-verb trio doubles as a repeatable mnemonic for word-of-mouth. Cohesion pass: reworded to felt-outcome language so §3 no longer echoes §2's mechanism words ["fast modern site", "day-to-day runs itself"] — §2 routes, §3 inspires.)*

**Lead-out:** You don't need to know which of these you need — working that out is the first thing we do together, and I'll only ever suggest what genuinely earns its place.
**CTA:** → Let's work out your fit  *(softened from "Book a free discovery call" so §6 owns the hard close — avoids duplicate CTAs)*

**Design principle (carry to Solutions rewrite too):** possibility, not paralysis — collapse long menus into a few felt outcomes, then take the burden of choosing off the visitor and hand it to the discovery conversation. The 6 current Solutions cards all nest inside these 3 buckets.

## 4. How it works — APPROVED

**Heading:** How it works
**Sub:** Four steps. No jargon, no surprises, no being left in the dark.

1. **Discover** — We start with a free 30-minute call about your business and what's slowing it down. No obligation, no pressure — and the more you can share beforehand, the more I can tailor it to you *(longer if we're onto something).*
2. **Plan** — I map out exactly what I'd build and what it costs, *before* any work starts. You approve it — nothing comes as a surprise.
3. **Build** — I build it with you in the loop the whole way. You're a partner in it, not handed a black box at the end.
4. **Support** — I don't vanish at launch. I stick around to keep things running and adapt as your business grows.

*(Step "Design" → "Plan" to stay pillar-neutral and avoid clashing with the Web & Design pillar name. Each step deliberately answers a buyer fear: cost/scope, being lost, being ghosted post-launch.)*
