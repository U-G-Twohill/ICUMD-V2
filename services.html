How We Work

From first conversation to long-term support — here's what working together actually looks like.

Step 1

Discover

A free, no-pressure conversation about your business, your goals, and what isn't working right now. By the end of it you'll have a clear picture of what's possible, and a rough idea of cost and timeline.

Step 2

Plan

I write up exactly what I'd build and what it costs, before any work starts. You'll see wireframes and mockups before a line of code is written, and nothing goes ahead until you're happy with the direction.

Step 3

Build

I build it with you in the loop the whole way — regular updates and a staging link you can check any time, not a reveal at the end. Then I handle the launch, and walk you through everything so you're confident running it yourself.

Ongoing

Support

Your project doesn't end at launch. You'll get training, documentation, and full ownership of everything, so you're never dependent on me. If you'd rather not think about updates and upkeep at all, ongoing support is available from $200/month — and one-off help is fine too.

What Every Client Gets

Direct Communication

You work directly with the founder. No account managers, no middle-people.

Full Documentation

Written guides, video walkthroughs, and training so you're never left guessing.

No Lock-In

Your code, your content, your domain, and admin access to everything. If you ever want to move it elsewhere, I'll hand over what's needed — no hostage-taking.

Mobile-First

Performance Optimised

Genuine Care
