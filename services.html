Services

At ICU Media Design, we offer a range of services designed to meet the unique needs of small & medium businesses in New Zealand. Our focus areas include web design, maintenance, and additional features that enhance your online presence.

Web & Design

Transform your online presence with our comprehensive web services. We create stunning, responsive websites from concept to launch, complete with professional hosting and ongoing maintenance. Our packages include everything you need to succeed online - from custom branding and visual design to SEO optimization, analytics integration, and fully-featured e-commerce stores. Whether you're starting fresh or refreshing your existing site, we handle the technical details so you can focus on your business.

Solutions

Streamline your business operations with intelligent automation solutions tailored to your needs. From simple time-savers like automated lead capture and calendar scheduling to sophisticated custom-built systems, we help eliminate repetitive tasks and improve efficiency. Our solutions range from connecting your existing tools to work seamlessly together, to developing cutting-edge features like AI-powered chatbots that enhance customer experience. Let technology work smarter for you, not harder.
