Solutions & Automations

At ICU Media Design, we offer a range of services designed to meet the unique needs of small & medium businesses in New Zealand. Our focus areas include web design, maintenance, and additional features that enhance your online presence.

Smarter Business. Less Manual Work.

We help businesses run more smoothly with smart solutions tailored to their unique needs. From discovering bottlenecks to building powerful automations and integrations, our step-by-step process ensures you get results — not just tools.

Discovery & Planning

We learn how your business works and where it can improve.

Tool Selection & Setup

We find the right tools, connect them, and build the right automations.

Ongoing Support

We help you improve and adapt as your business evolves.
Integrations

Connect your tools and eliminate busywork. We integrate your website and business systems with the platforms you already use — from calendars and CRMs to marketing tools and chatbots. That means smoother operations and no more double-handling.

Calendar & Booking

Sync your website forms with Google Calendar, Calendly, or other tools.

CRM & Email Platforms

Connect your website with Mailchimp, HubSpot, Notion, or ConvertKit.

Spreadsheets & Forms

Auto-fill Google Sheets from site forms or sync with databases.

Custom Chatbots

Deploy on-site chatbots that answer questions or guide users intelligently.

Marketing & Social Tools

Connect signup forms to email campaigns or automate social content posting.
Automations

Let technology do the heavy lifting. We design custom automations that streamline your business processes — from lead capture to notifications, scheduling, content generation, and more. Free up time and reduce errors with workflows that run themselves.

Lead Capture Flows

Automatically send follow-ups and update your CRM when someone contacts you.

Booking Confirmations

Trigger emails, tasks, or Slack alerts whenever a new booking is made.

Content Automation

Automatically publish blog posts, newsletters, or generate content summaries.

Task Reminders

Set up automations to handle recurring tasks, reminders, and admin work.

AI Agents

Use intelligent AI assistants to summarize, suggest, or automate workflows.
