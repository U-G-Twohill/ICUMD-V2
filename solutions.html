Common Questions About Solutions & Automations

What kinds of business tasks can you automate?

Lead follow-ups, booking confirmations, email campaigns, content scheduling, invoice reminders, data syncing between tools, and much more. If you're doing it manually and repeatedly, there's probably a smarter way.

Do I need a new website to use your solutions service?

No. Our solutions work with any existing website. We can also pair them with a new build if you're starting from scratch — that's where Design and Solutions work best together.

How much do your solutions cost?

It depends on complexity. Simple integrations start from $300 setup, automations from $500. Monthly management is also available. We'll scope everything in a free discovery call — no surprises.

What tools and platforms do you integrate with?

Google Workspace, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Calendly, Stripe, Xero, Slack, Notion, Zapier, Make, and many more. If it has an API, we can probably connect it.
